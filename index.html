Popular Destinations

New York City

Visit the city that never sleeps and discoveer its iconic landmarks, world-class museums, and diverse neighborhoods.

Paris

Paris is home to several iconic landmarks, including the Eiffel Tower, which is one of the most recognizable structures in the world. Other famous landmarks include the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Arc de Triomphe, and the Champs-Élysées.

Tokyo

Tokyo is at the forefront of technological advancements and is often considered a hub of innovation. The city is home to numerous electronics stores, tech companies, and research institutes.

The Plaza Hotel
